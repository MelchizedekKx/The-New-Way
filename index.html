Ritz Cracker Chicken Casserole

Blooming Delight of Many Choices

 

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 3/4 Cottage Cheese
  • 3/4 Sour Cream
  • 1/4 Sliced chives
  • 3/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 Cayenne Pepper
  • 4c. cooked shredded chicken
  • 1c. frozen peas, thawed freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2c. shredded cheddar
  • 31 Ritz crackers (from 1 sleeve, crushed about 1 1/3c.)
  • 3 Tbsp. unsalted butter, melted Chopped fresh parsely, for serving (optional)
    • Directions

    Step 1- Preheat to 400 Degrees. Lightly grease an 11 x 8 baking dish with cooking spray.In a large bowl, whisk soup, cottage cheese, sour cream chives, garlic powder,and cayenne until smooth. Fold in chicken and peas: season with black pepper. Transfer to prepared baking dish and smooth in an even layer. Top with cheddar.

    Step 2- In a medium bowl, combine cracker crumbs and butter. Sprinkle over cheese.

    Step 3- Bake casserole until browned and bubbling, 25 to 30 minutes.

    Step 4- Let stand 5 minutes. Top with parsely (if using).
